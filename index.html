Rolly Bat

Type of Game
12-16yrs/2-50
Active
Outside
large
Stimulation:
Rating:
Source:
Added By:

Requirments

  • An outside area large enough to hit a baseball safely for the age group
  • a bat
  • a ball
  • optionally: baseball gloves

Directions

Pitcher pitches the ball to the batter until the batter hits the ball. If a fielder catches the ball in the air that person either gets to bat next or choose someone to bat next. If the batted ball touches the ground and the batter can touch it with the bat before a fielder gets to it, the batter can bat again. If a fielder fields the ball, she/she stays in the spot with the ball and the batter places the bat on the ground parallel to the fielder. The fielder then rolls or throws the ball to try to hit the bat. If he/she does that person gets to bat or choose the next person to bat.

Adaptations

To make it harder the batter can swing the bat like a pendulum. To make it easier the group can decide to give the fielder an x number of steps forward according to age or height or whatever the group decides. For very young children try using a whiffel bat and a beach ball and having them hit something much larger like the batter standing still.(with a beach ball not a hard ball)

Restrictions

Requires lots of movement and good hand-eye coordination.
